Benefits of Bento Club

Healthy Ingredients

We source all of our ingredients from local farmers. Along with fresh ingredients, we use nutritionist-approved recipes to help create balanced meals tailored to kids.

Save Time

Making kids lunches can be one of the most tedious and time-consuming things you do. Allow us to do all of the dirty work so you can spend more time in your happy place.

Sustainable Practices

We want to keep the world healthy just like your kids. We use sustainablity practices in everything we do, from food to packaging. We use 100% recyclable materials.

How It Works

1

Order Online

Every week you choose the meals your kids want from our online menu. You can mix and match to get exactly what your kids will love.

2

Delivery

You can choose from a list of delivery times. Our team of drivers deliver your meals every Sunday to maximize freshness.

3

Prep

On each day, three minutes of prep is required to take the pre-maid lunches and transfer them to the Bento Club Container. This keeps the food as fresh as possible without using harmful preservatives.
